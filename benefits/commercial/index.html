Commercial Terms & Partnership

Our commercial framework is designed for transparency and simplicity, ensuring we're aligned on goals while protecting both parties' interests.

1. Confidentiality & Trust

  • NDA Protection: We begin with a mutual non-disclosure agreement to securely handle your sensitive information
  • Data Security: Your details, strategies, and business information remain protected
  • Trust-Based Collaboration: We operate as your European extension with complete transparency

2. Commercial Framework

  • Success-Based Model: We earn commission only on actual sales - aligned with your success
  • Price Control: You set all sale prices and maintain full margin control
  • Transparent Costs: Clear breakdown of our commission and pass-through expenses
  • No Hidden Fees: Complete visibility into all operational costs

3. Payment Terms

  • Monthly Settlement: Regular payout cycle with monthly cut-off dates
  • Fast Transfers: Funds credited within maximum 2 working weeks after month-end
  • SEPA Transfers: Direct bank transfers to your nominated IBAN account
  • Detailed Reporting: Comprehensive settlement reports with full cost breakdown

4. Compliance & Verification

  • Basic KYC: Simple verification only when required by platforms, typically once
  • EU Compliance: We ensure all operations meet platform policies and EU regulations
  • Document Management: We handle the administrative burden of compliance paperwork

5. Partnership Flexibility

  • Adaptable Arrangements: Terms that evolve with your business needs
  • Easy Transitions: Simple processes for pausing or concluding operations
  • Focused on Growth: Structure designed to support your European expansion

6. Why Our Terms Work

  • Aligned Success: We only succeed when you succeed in European markets
  • No Long-Term Lock-in: Flexible partnership based on performance
  • Mutual Growth: Our partnership scales with your European sales
  • Complete Transparency: Clear expectations and open communication

Why Our Terms Work

Our commercial terms reflect our core principle: we build lasting partnerships through transparent agreements and shared success in European markets.

FAQ - Commercial Terms & Partnership

How does your pricing model work? ▼

We operate on a success-based commission model - we only earn when you make sales. You maintain full price control and margin, and we provide complete transparency on all costs.

What are your payment terms and settlement frequency? ▼

We provide monthly settlements with funds transferred via SEPA within maximum 2 working weeks after month-end, accompanied by detailed reports showing all costs and commissions.

Do you require long-term contracts or commitments? ▼

No - we operate on flexible terms without long-term lock-in. Our partnership scales with your success and can be adjusted based on performance.

How do you protect our confidential business information? ▼

We start with mutual NDAs and maintain strict data security protocols to protect your pricing strategies, sales data, and business information.

What costs should we expect beyond your commission? ▼

You'll see transparent breakdowns of marketplace fees, shipping costs, and other pass-through expenses - no hidden fees, complete visibility into all operational costs.

How do you handle compliance and administrative requirements? ▼

We manage the administrative burden of compliance paperwork and ensure all operations meet EU regulations and platform policies.

What's the process if we need to pause or adjust our partnership? ▼

We maintain simple processes for pausing or concluding operations, with flexible arrangements that adapt to your changing business needs.

Do we need to complete extensive verification processes? ▼

We only require basic KYC verification when platforms mandate it, typically just once during initial setup.

How does your model ensure our interests are aligned? ▼

Since we only earn when you succeed in European markets, our growth is directly tied to your sales performance - creating true partnership alignment.

What makes your commercial terms different from other providers? ▼

Complete transparency, no long-term lock-in, success-based pricing, and flexible terms that actually support your European growth journey.
